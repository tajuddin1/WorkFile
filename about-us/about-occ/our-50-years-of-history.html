Our 50 Years of History

Our Milestones

1969 - 1976

1969

Chemist Heinz Suter developed ORO-CLEAN in his fatherâ€™s garage, a phenol-free cleaning agent for dental suction units.

1969

Foundation of the company "Dentalchemie Heinz Suter" in KÃ¼snacht, to market the cleaner ORO-CLEAN.

1969

Successful distribution of ORO-CLEAN by leading Swiss dental dealers.

1976

Expansion of the product portfolion through the development and market introduction of the instrument disinfectant Uni-Clean.

1977 - 1978

1977

Change of the company's legal form to a limited liability company and its renaming to Oro Clean Chemie AG.

1977

Move to larger business premises in Wetzikon.

1977

Launch of DENTIRO, a fast-acting surface disinfectant specifically designed to combat hepatitis B viruses.

1978

Expansion of the product range with the instrument disinfectant Self-Clean and introduction of 10-litre canisters.

1980 - 1990

1980

Introduction of DENTIRO FeuchttÃ¼cher, fast-acting disinfectant wipes with a broad effective spectrum and a short contact time.

1980

First-time participation in an international trade fair, located in Nuremberg, Germany.

1984

Construction of own business premises in PfÃ¤ffikon ZH.

1986

Reformulation of the instrument disinfectant Uni-Clean and relaunch as OROCID-MULTISEPT Plus, the first fast-acting instrument disinfectant developed in Switzerland that is effective against hepatitis B viruses.

1988

Introduction of the PROSEPT brand, developed specifically for hospitals and medical practices.

1991 - 1996

1991

Collaboration with the University of Zurich to develop Turbocidol, a cleaning and disinfectant product for the use with the Turbocid device. One of the first devices that automatically cleaned, disinfected and lubricated dental hand-pieces, contra-angles and turbines.

1996

Successful certification according to EN 46001 as the first Swiss dental company.

1998 - 2005

1998

Relocation to the newly built business and production premises in Fehraltorf.

2002

Takeover of the company by Ursula Suter.

2004

Introduction of the extremely economical disinfectant ASEPTOPRINT Liquid for dental impressions.

2006 - 2015

2006

Expansion of business and production premises in Fehraltorf.

2007

Expansion of analysis and microbiology laboratory with state-of-the-art instruments and equipment.

2009

Complete overhaul of the corporate design.

2009

Development and marketing of OROLIN Perasept, a bactericidal, fungicidal, virucidal and sporicidal product for the cold sterilisation of heat-sensitive instruments such as endoscopes.

2011

Development and marketing of OROLIN Intra, a brand-new, patented system for manually disinfecting, cleaning and lubricating hand-pieces and contra-angles.

2016 - 2023

2016

Incorporation of Oro Clean Chemie Sdn. Bhd. and opening of 600 m2 premises in the centre of Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.

2017

Incorporation of Oro Clean Chemie s.r.o. in Prague, Czech Republic.
