From day one, our mission has been to produce disinfectants that are highly effective, environmentally safe and compatible with a wide range of materials. This commitment remains unwavering. Surface Disinfectants

We manufactures a wide range of alcohol-based and alcohol-free surface disinfectants for the quick disinfection and decontamination of surfaces. We not only produce ready-to-use solutions and disinfectant wipes but also disinfectant concentrates that are more economical and therefore suitable for the disinfection and cleaning of large areas such as floors or sanitary installations.

EXPLORE OUR SURFACE DISINFECTANTS ›

Instruments Disinfectants and Cleaners

We manufactures range of high-quality instrument disinfectants and ultrasonic cleaners to reprocess instruments quickly, easily and most importantly safely.
