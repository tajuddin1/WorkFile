Infection Control Trainings

OCC Switzerland offers a number of online and on-site infection control training courses tailboard to the needs of today's healthcare professionals. Our infection control training courses are designed for professionals in various fields who have a working knowledge of the infection control and patient to their job responsibilities. We offer customised on-site training courses that are free of charge.

On Site Training

Our personalised on-site training gives practice staff a thorough understanding of safe and effective infection control procedures. The infection control training also examines the reasons certain procedures need to be performed and explores the risk inherent in dentistry.The highly interactive training session encourages staff participation and make compliance easy.
Fill in the details below and we will get back to you as soon as possible.

Online Training

If one-site training is not an option for you, we offer several online infection control training modules. Each of our online training modules lasts for 10 minutes or less, making them the perfect option for time-strapped professionals. The online session will be conducted via Microsoft Teams.
